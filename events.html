REPUBLIC DAY

On January 26, 2025, Republic Day celebrations took place at 8 AM, marking the 75th anniversary of India's Constitution. The event featured a grand flag hoisting ceremony, patriotic performances, and cultural programs, highlighting India's rich heritage and diversity. It was a moment of national pride, with citizens across the country joining in to celebrate India's democratic values and achievements.

Around 150 villag students attended
Around 30 IIIT students managed the event
